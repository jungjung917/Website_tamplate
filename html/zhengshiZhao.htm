Research

Blue stragglers stars

My research aimed to derive a comprehensive interpretation for origins of BSS by investigating the BSS properties in different star systems, such as open clusters, globular clusters and dwarf spheroidal galaxies based on observational data of the highest quality up to the present day.

Solar analogue stars

Study of solar analogs is fundamental for a better understanding of the Sun and of the solar system. Moreover, they can be used to find solar twin stars and their abundances provide interesting insight to the study of galactic chemical evolution and the formation of extra-solar planetary systems.
